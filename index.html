A day in the life of a Groupe employee

Publicis Groupe has reinvented itself for what employee satisfaction is all about.

Publicis Groupe is famous for its main office complex, which is basically a playground for adults. With great company, it's a place most people only dream of working at.

The overall configuration of your office should also be taken into account. For example, if teamwork is one of your most treasured values, you're hurting yourself if your office is nothing but a series of cubicles that cut team members off from one another unlike the Groupe's offices around the globe.

  1. Ability to learn new things

    Limitless number of oportunities to grow and expand your knowledge.

  2. Feeling of personal appreciation

    No matter how hard you work; you will always be appreciated within team members and other employees as well

  3. Sense of belonging

    During work hours, not only do you feel at home, but the balance between one's work and mental state is quite interesting

  4. Fair pay for job

    A lot of companies do not compensate their employees as to what they deserve, in which it returns lots of attritions and employees leaving. Here, it is not the case

  5. Teamwork

    Nothing is set to overcome alone; should it be a complicated challenge or a simple task

  6. Trust in colleagues

    What good does it do if team members do not have trust between themselves? Personally and professionally, the workspace is very healthy and not toxic at all!

Before diving into a day in the life of a Groupe Employee, we should add two disclaimers: Obviously, the job varies day-to-day. Also, every company has its own culture and quirks
