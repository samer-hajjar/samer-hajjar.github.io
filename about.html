What is Publicis Groupe?

Founded in 1926, Publicis Groupe is today the third world communication group. Thanks to a powerful alchemy of creativity and technology, we drive business transformation across the entire value chain.

Publicis Groupe is organized into 4 Solutions poles: Publicis Communications, Publicis Media, Publicis Sapient and Publicis Health for better connectivity between its services and perfect integration of its offer. In this organization, all brands continue to exist and share the same services, giving them the power and expertise of all areas of Solutions combined to face competition on a large scale and win new customers in a driven world. by data. Epsilon, the data and technology platform, and its Conversant division joined the Group in July 2019. Placed at the heart of the Group's activities, it is accelerating the implementation of the Power of One thanks to its incomparable expertise.
